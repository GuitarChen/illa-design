文字排版

字体排版被用于展示文本信息，主要由三个子组件构成：

  • Heading 标题：多层级展示文本内容
  • Text 文本：丰富的文本展示样式
  • Paragraph 段落 ：设置多行内容的布局

安装​

yarn add @illa-design/typography

引用组件​

import { Typography } from "@illa-design/typography"

组件接口(API)​

参数名描述类型默认值

ellipsisConfig​

参数名描述类型默认值

使用方法​

基础用法​

<Typograph>
  <Heading>I am a title</Heading>
  <Paragraph>I am a paragraph</Paragraph>
  <Text>I am text</Text>
</Typograph>

文本样式​

<Text>
  ILLA Design
</Text>
<Text colorScheme="red">
  ILLA Design
</Text>
<Text bold>
  Bold
</Text>
<Text disabled>
  Disabled
</Text>
<Text mark>
  Mark
</Text>
<Text underline>
  Underline
</Text>
<Text delete>
  Line through
</Text>
<Text code>
  Code snippet
</Text>

段落缩进​

<paragraph indent>
  I took her hand in mine, and we went out of the ruined place; and, as the morning mists had risen long ago when I first left the forge, so, the evening mists were rising now, and in all the broad expanse of tranquil light they showed to me, I saw no shadow of another parting from her.
</paragraph>

省略​

<paragraph indent ellipsis={new EllipsisBuilder().rows(2).expandable(true).create()}>
  I took her hand in mine, and we went out of the ruined place; and, as the morning mists had risen long ago when I first left the forge, so, the evening mists were rising now, and in all the broad expanse of tranquil light they showed to me, I saw no shadow of another parting from her.
</paragraph>
